Considering Computer Science in College
Computer Science is one of the most in-demand and versatile degrees you can earn today. But before you jump in, it’s worth asking: Is it the right fit for you?
1. What college has to offer
College offers not only a way to learn Computer Science, but also provides a platform to launch your career. College is a fun way to learn and test your skills while participating in the college life. There are many opportunities to connect with other like minded people and make friends for life.
2. Is college right for you?
College is a great choice, but maybe not the best choice for everyone. A degree can get you very far in your career, but does set you back financially. There are many different ways to aid you in this endeavor, including donations, grants, and scholarships But you can always learn on your own, especially if you are driven.
3. What college should I go to?
Picking a college is one of the most important parts in your journey. For Computer Science, prioritixing their CS degree is a must. From there, you can pick what fits you! Locations, environment, difficulty, and general vibes are all extremely important!
